Services

What Can I Do For You?

Whatever the task at hand, I have a service to assist you.

Web-Based Testing

I can review and throughly test your website or web-based application for useability, bugs, and accessibility for users with disabilities. Looking for another opinion and review prior to going live? Let me help you today and ensure peace of mind!

Smartphone App Testing

I maintain a variety of android devices (phones and tablets) for smartphone application testing. Interested in testing on Apple devices as well? I will utilize emulators to ensure total coverage.

Accessibility Testing

Would you like to ensure that your creation reaches the widest audience possible? Testing to ensure that your software is viewable for those with colorblindness, or works well with screen readers for the blind, is of utmost importance. Let me examine your software from a different perspective.

Performance Testing

Your website is finally ready for release, but can it flawlessly handle the level of concurrent users you forsee? Let me check for you, utilizing performance testing tools such a Jmeter.

Manual Test Case Writing

If you have already assembled a group of alpha or beta testers, but would like a list of test cases for them to run through, I would be happy to be of service. I can review your software/website's specifications, and create a manual testing suite for your company's use.
